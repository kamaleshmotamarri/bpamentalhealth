Anxiety

Excessive worry or fear that interferes with daily life.

Depression

Persistent sadness and loss of interest in activities.

Bipolar Disorder

Extreme mood swings including emotional highs and lows.

PTSD

Schizophrenia

OCD

Eating Disorders

Serious conditions related to persistent eating behaviors.

ADHD

Difficulty paying attention, hyperactivity and impulsive behavior.
