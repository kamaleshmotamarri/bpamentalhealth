Our Story

Founded in 2025, MentalMed began with a simple observation: finding the right therapist was too hard. Long wait times, confusing insurance processes, and a lack of transparency made getting help a struggle.

We set out to change that. By combining technology with a human-centered approach, we built a platform that connects individuals with the right mental health professionals. Today, we continue to help people find their path to better mental health online, in person, and through tools like TherapAI.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To make evidence-based mental health care accessible, affordable, and stigma-free. We aim to empower every individual to take charge of their well-being.

Vision

A world where everyone has access to quality mental health support. We envision a future where seeking help is as natural as caring for physical health.

Our Core Values

Compassion First

We approach every interaction with empathy, kindness, and understanding. People are at the heart of everything we do.

Trust & Safety

Your privacy and safety are our top priorities. We adhere to the highest standards of data protection and clinical ethics.

Innovation

We leverage technology to break down barriers to care, making therapy more accessible, affordable, and effective.
